# Persona: The Mobile Glancer

**Name:** Priya
**Age:** 25
**Occupation:** UX designer
**Primary series:** F1, Formula E

## About

Priya checks RaceTrack on her phone during commutes or while waiting. She's digitally savvy and appreciates clean design. She follows F1 closely and Formula E with interest. She doesn't sit at a desktop — this is a mobile-first experience for her.

## Goals

- Quick glance at what's happening this week
- See how far the season has progressed
- Save events to watchlist so she remembers to tune in

## Pain Points

- Dense card layouts are hard to scan on a 390px screen
- Tiny tap targets for fav buttons cause mis-taps
- Long text-heavy pages require too much scrolling on mobile

## How She Uses RaceTrack

- Checks dashboard on the go, usually scrolls to the hero countdown
- Taps a card to open the modal for session times
- Adds events to watchlist to keep track
- Might use the calendar page to plan a few weeks ahead

## Design Implications

- Cards must be finger-friendly — fav button needs adequate tap target (44px min)
- Dashboard should front-load the most important info above the fold
- Calendar grid needs to work at 390px — use compact day cells with just color dots
- Navigation must be thumb-reachable on small screens
- Load performance matters — she's often on mobile data
