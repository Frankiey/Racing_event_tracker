## Series Overview

The IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship — America's premier sports car endurance series. ~12 rounds per season including the Rolex 24 at Daytona and Petit Le Mans. Mix of sprint races and endurance events (2.5h to 24h). Multi-class: GTD Pro, GTD, LMP2, GTP.
